Introduction

Perfect Message ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Message Preferences: Your selected relationship types, tones, and other options used to generate personalized messages.
  • Subscription Information: Payment and subscription status managed through Apple's App Store.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Generate personalized messages based on your preferences
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve our app and services
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage & Security

We prioritize your data security. Message preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store the content of generated messages on our servers. All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management and payments
  • RevenueCat: For subscription analytics and management
  • AI Services: For generating personalized message content

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
