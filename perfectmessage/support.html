Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a message?

Simply select your relationship type, tone, and other preferences, then tap "Generate Message" to create a personalized message.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your device's App Store settings under Subscriptions.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime through your App Store subscription settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
